Enchanted by the Sun

I once read amidst the soar and glide of the flock of hummingbirds
Envisage the peacefulness of this amusing ambiance of earth
Starring at the clear blue sky
Words that I read are running through my mind

Then I saw a glimpse of this splendid sun
My gaze is locked, I stray but I can't,
Words before me, yet I concede,
I cannot focus, nor take heed.

How could I turn my gaze from thee,
Fair sun, that bids my heart to flee,
Thy beauty bright, a sight to see,
Entranced am I, in reverie.

Though the skies were clear,
Sun's bright that produce warm air,
Love's bolt did strike,
A sudden flash, transforming life.

At that moment, my heart knew,
As the sun appeared in view,
Love's sweet arrow struck me true,
And in its light, my soul anew.

The day began in brilliant light,
But later, the sun gave way to night.
Lost in the past, the present unknown,
Will sunlight's warmth again be shown?

Like a cherished song, forever in tune,
In my thoughts, your memory strewn.
Like the moon that waxes and wanes,
Our paths may diverge and meet again.

In the depths of my heart, forever true,
My love for you will always imbue.
Through distance, time, and circumstance,
My heart still longs for our next dance.
